The first Olympic Games were held in Greece almost 3,000 years ago in 776 BC. Athletes gathered in Olympia to honor the god Zeus.

  • Women: Not allowed to compete or watch if married.
  • Uniform: Male athletes competed entirely naked.
  • Prizes: Winners received an olive branch.

The modern games began in 1894, with the first modern event held in Athens in 1896.
